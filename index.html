Our purpose

A wraparound quality-of-life service for organisations supporting people across care, education and health.

Our central mission is to improve quality of life for those receiving support and for the people, teams and services providing it. We bring training, consultancy, reflective practice and evidence-based support together so organisations can build safer, more capable and more compassionate environments.

About QOLCOE

Changing how care and support are delivered.

At the Quality of Life Centre of Excellence, we combine real understanding, positive behaviour support and proven wellbeing approaches to help people, teams and organisations create environments where everyone can thrive.

Our work is built for practice: thoughtful training, clear consultation and support that moves beyond tick-box compliance into confident, compassionate action.
