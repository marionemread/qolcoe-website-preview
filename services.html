All-Wales e-learning

Accessible digital learning for safer, more compassionate practice.

Developed with Swansea Bay University Health Board and co-produced with experts by experience, these courses support professionals and family carers across health, education and social care.

Improvement Cymru supported

Reducing Restrictive Practice E-Learning Course

Informed by national policy and international initiatives, this course raises awareness of restrictive practices and supports safer, trauma-informed approaches to care.

Increase knowledge of restrictive practices and their impact on individuals.
Understand the legal context, safeguards and human rights considerations.
Introduce alternative strategies that reduce the use of restrictive practices.
Promote safer, more compassionate and trauma-informed care.
Access course on NHS Wales
Regional Integration Fund supported

Positive Behaviour Support Awareness E-Learning Course

Built to increase awareness of PBS, this course explores values, behaviour as communication, unmet needs, and relational support through a trauma-informed lens.

Understand what Positive Behaviour Support is and its twelve key components.
Explore the core values that underpin PBS practice.
Learn the science behind behaviours of concern as communication.
Apply PBS strategies through trauma-informed and relational thinking.
Access course on NHS Wales

Both courses are also available as All-Wales courses on ESR.
